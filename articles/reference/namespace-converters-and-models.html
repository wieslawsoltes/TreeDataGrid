Guidance

Use these types when building internal infrastructure for custom sources, rows, and columns.

  • Prefer NotifyingBase for simple INotifyPropertyChanged models.
  • Use NotifyingListBase<T> when range notifications are required.
  • Use ReadOnlyListBase<T> for adapter-style list projections.
